How many ways are there to choose two distinct positive integers totaling
N
, disregarding the order?

1 \leq N \leq 10^6

Input
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
N
Output
Print the answer.

4
Sample Output 1
1

4
: to choose
1
and
3
. (Choosing
3
and
1
is not considered different from this.)

999999

499999
